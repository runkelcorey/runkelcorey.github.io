Who

Corey Runkel is a Research Associate at the Yale Program on Financial Stability and Tobin Center Pre-Doctoral Fellow. He’s interested in everything under the sun, though some things under the sun—political economy, regional governance, and communication, for instance—have held his interest longer.

Corey uses this website to exercise his writing muscles, document his work, and spew the thoughts that occur to him while cooking pasta, climbing rocks, and riding his bike.
